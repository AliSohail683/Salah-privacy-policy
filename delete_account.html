How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your SalahOS account directly from the app or by contacting us via email.

Option 1: Delete from the App

  1. Open SalahOS and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to the Settings screen (accessible from the Home tab).
  3. Scroll down to the Account section.
  4. Tap "Delete Account".
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted. You will be asked to confirm twice to prevent accidental deletion.

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us. Please include the email address associated with your SalahOS account.

Request Account Deletion

We will process your request within 30 days.

What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

Data Type Action
Account profile (email, display name, timezone) Permanently deleted
Prayer logs and daily scores Permanently deleted
Streak data (current, longest, Fajr streak) Permanently deleted
Group memberships Removed from all groups
Notification preferences and FCM token Permanently deleted
AI Coach feedback history Permanently deleted
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your prayer history, streaks, and group memberships will be lost. If you create a new account later, you will start from scratch.
Data stored only on your device (cached prayer times, theme preferences) is not affected by account deletion. You can clear this data by uninstalling the app.
