1. Introduction

Welcome to Salah Deen ("the App," "we," "us," or "our"). Salah Deen is an Islamic prayer companion application that helps Muslim users track their five daily prayers (Salah), find Qibla direction, maintain prayer streaks, join accountability groups, and receive personalised coaching feedback based on their prayer habits.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and your rights regarding your data. By using Salah Deen, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

Summary: We collect only what is necessary to provide the core features of the App. We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for advertising. Location data is used solely to calculate prayer times and Qibla direction.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you create an account and use the App:

2.1 Account & Identity Information

  • Email address – required to create and log into your account.
  • Display name – the name shown to other group members.
  • Password – handled entirely by Firebase Authentication; we never store or have access to your plaintext password.
  • Timezone – automatically detected from your device at registration to ensure prayer times are displayed in your local time.

2.2 Location Data

  • GPS coordinates (latitude & longitude) – collected when you open the App and when you use the Qibla Compass feature.
  • City label – a human-readable city name derived from your coordinates, stored for display purposes only.

Your coordinates are used exclusively to (a) fetch accurate daily prayer times from the Aladhan API and (b) calculate the Qibla bearing on your device. Your precise GPS coordinates are cached in our database linked to the date, so that prayer times do not need to be re-fetched if you remain in the same area. We recalculate only when you move more than approximately 2.5 km. Location data is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any purpose other than prayer-time and Qibla calculations.

2.3 Prayer & Religious Practice Data

  • Daily prayer log – your completion status for each of the five prayers (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha): on-time, late, missed, or unmarked.
  • Daily prayer score – a numerical score calculated from your statuses (0–100).
  • Streak data – current streak, longest streak, Fajr-specific streak, and total days prayed.
  • Madhab preference (optional) – your Islamic jurisprudence school (Hanafi, Shafii, Maliki, or Hanbali), used only to adjust prayer time calculation methods.

2.4 Notification & Device Data

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token – a device-specific push notification token used to send prayer reminders and group pings to your device.
  • Device platform – iOS or Android, recorded alongside your FCM token.
  • Notification preferences – per-prayer settings including whether reminders are enabled, how many minutes before prayer to notify you, and the alert sound level.

2.5 Group & Social Data

  • Group membership – if you create or join an accountability group, your display name and daily prayer statuses are visible to other members of that group.
  • Invite codes – 6-character codes used to join groups; these are not linked to personal identity.

2.6 Usage & Analytics Data

  • Weekly prayer analytics – aggregated breakdown of your prayer statuses across a 7-day window, used to generate personalised coaching feedback.
  • Offline sync queue – pending prayer status updates stored temporarily on your device when you are offline, synced automatically when connectivity resumes.

2.7 Data Stored Only on Your Device (Not Transmitted)

  • Cached prayer times for offline access.
  • Cached daily log for offline access.
  • App theme preference (light/dark).

4. Device Permissions

The App requests the following permissions. All permissions are explained at the time of request:

5. Third-Party Services

Salah Deen integrates the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy that governs their data practices:

5.1 Google Firebase (Google LLC)

We use multiple Firebase products to power the App's backend:

  • Firebase Authentication – manages secure sign-up and sign-in using email and password. Firebase hashes and stores passwords; we never see your plaintext password.
  • Cloud Firestore – our primary database that stores your account profile, prayer logs, streak data, group membership, notification preferences, and prayer times cache.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) – delivers push notifications (prayer reminders and group pings) to your device.
  • Firebase Cloud Functions – server-side functions that generate personalised AI coaching feedback based on your weekly prayer analytics, and that send group ping notifications.

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5.2 Aladhan Prayer Times API

Prayer times are fetched from the Aladhan API (aladhan.com). When requesting prayer times, the App sends your latitude, longitude, and calculation method to the Aladhan API. No user identity or account information is included in these requests.

Aladhan Privacy Policy: https://aladhan.com/privacy-policy

5.3 Expo Updates (Expo / Microsoft)

Salah Deen uses Expo Updates to deliver over-the-air (OTA) app updates without requiring a full App Store or Play Store release. Expo may collect anonymous crash and update telemetry data.

Expo Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

6. Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore, which is hosted in Google's secure cloud infrastructure. Google applies industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit (TLS/HTTPS) to all Firestore data.

Additional security measures we apply:

  • Firebase Authentication tokens are used to authenticate all database requests, ensuring that each user can only read and write their own data.
  • Firestore Security Rules restrict access to documents based on the authenticated user's UID.
  • Passwords are never stored by us; they are managed entirely by Firebase Authentication using industry-standard hashing.
  • All communication between the App and our backend is encrypted over HTTPS.

While we implement strong security measures, no system is completely immune to breaches. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify affected users in accordance with applicable law.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Specifically:

  • Account and profile data – retained until you delete your account.
  • Prayer logs and streak data – retained until you delete your account.
  • Prayer times cache – retained for up to 1 year on a rolling basis; older entries are periodically purged.
  • FCM push tokens – retained until you sign out, uninstall the App, or your token is rotated by the device operating system.
  • Group data – a group and its data are deleted when the last member leaves.

When you delete your account, we will permanently delete all personally identifiable data associated with your account from our servers within 30 days.
